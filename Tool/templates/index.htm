About Us

Organiziva helps you in prioritizing your tasks in an organised way while taking into consideration the mental fatigue that employees may suffer from while working remotely.

Sign up now and plan out your tasks with Organiziva for free.

Work together

Make your own team or join a new one by getting the id from the owner. Remember: Only the owner (the person who has created the team) can add new events to the team's database. Team members can work on the given task and change its status as the work progresses.

What's New?



Help Your Team-mates

We present you our Knowledge Panel where you can post information to help your team members out. The 'StackOverflow' of your company is here. Interns and new employees can come here to get information about any task or project.

Remote Working

With remote working being the new 'normal', we bring to you our 'Rent' feature where you can post your items for renting to people. You can go through the items already in the database and contact the owner of that item to rent it if you want to.
